About Us

Established in 2011 under the Anjuman-I-Islam Trust, the School of Engineering and Technology at Kalsekar Technical Campus, Navi Mumbai, is approved by AICTE and affiliated with the University of Mumbai. The institute offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and Ph.D. programs across seven engineering disciplines, along with a B.Sc. (IT) course. The campus is equipped with modern infrastructure and dedicated research facilities.
